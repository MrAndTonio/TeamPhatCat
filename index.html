There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Phat Cat. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring Web site addresses. This information is used by Phat Cat for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Phat Cat Web site.

Phat Cat is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on Web sites outside of the Phat Cat and Phat Cat family of Web sites.

Use of your Personal Information
 Phat Cat collects and uses your personal information to operate the Phat Cat Web site and deliver the services you have requested. Phat Cat also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Phat Cat and its affiliates. Phat Cat may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Phat Cat does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Phat Cat may, from time to time, contact you on behalf of external business partners about a particular offering that may be of interest to you. In those cases, your unique personally identifiable information (e-mail, name, address, telephone number) is not transferred to the third party. In addition, Phat Cat may share data with trusted partners to help us perform statistical analysis, send you email or postal mail, provide customer support, or arrange for deliveries. All such third parties are prohibited from using your personal information except to provide these services to Phat Cat, and they are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Phat Cat does not use or disclose sensitive personal information, such as race, religion, or political affiliations, without your explicit consent.

Phat Cat keeps track of the Web sites and pages our customers visit within Phat Cat, in order to determine what Phat Cat services are the most popular. This data is used to deliver customized content and advertising within Phat Cat to customers whose behavior indicates that they are interested in a particular subject area.

Phat Cat Web sites will disclose your personal information, without notice, only if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) conform to the edicts of the law or comply with legal process served on Phat Cat or the site; (b) protect and defend the rights or property of Phat Cat; and, (c) act under exigent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Phat Cat, or the public.

Use of Cookies
 The Phat Cat Web site use "cookies" to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Phat Cat pages, or register with Phat Cat site or services, a cookie helps Phat Cat to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Phat Cat Web site, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Phat Cat features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Phat Cat services or Web sites you visit.

Security of your Personal Information
 Phat Cat secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Phat Cat secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other Web sites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
